I've released Publ v0.8.8. It adds one new feature, namely the `user` object now has an [`is_bot`](733#is_bot) flag to indicate whether the site is being perused by a declared bot.

This functionality is particularly useful for implementing [crawler mitigations](210), which have been quite effective with the current onslaught of badly-behaved crawlers.

I have also finally begun to collect some [useful recipes](/manual/recipes/).
